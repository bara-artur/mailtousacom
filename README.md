## 📌 Project Overview
MailtoUsa is a comprehensive logistics management system designed for cross-border shipping from Canada to the United States. The platform automates the entire lifecycle of a shipment, including custom rate calculations, border crossing documentation, and multi-language client support (English & French).

The project consists of a high-load **Client Portal** and a robust **Admin/Manager Dashboard** integrated with a public-facing corporate website.

## 🚀 Key Functionality

### 1. Client Portal (User Dashboard)
* **Smart Shipping Form:** Step-by-step parcel entry with address auto-completion and real-time shipping cost calculation based on package dimensions and weight.
* **Bulk Operations:** Mass import of shipments via CSV/Excel, bulk status updates (Cancel/Ready for Shipping), and automated scheduling.
* **Financial Tools:** Automated generation and printing of custom invoices (weekly/monthly cycles) with payment status tracking.
* **Support System:** Integrated internal ticketing system for customer service.

### 2. Admin & Manager Control Panel
* **Logistics Automation:** One-click generation of **Border Crossing Manifests**, essential for international shipping compliance.
* **Dynamic Pricing Engine:** Ability to assign individual, custom tariffs and rates for specific users or groups.
* **Warehouse Management:** Bulk status management (Ready, Pending, In Transit, Return, Delivered) and temporary storage tracking.
* **Full Financial Control:** Management and audit of all client invoices and payment histories.

### 3. Public Corporate Website (CMS)
* Multilingual interface (English & French).
* Dynamic pricing tables and service descriptions.
* Full-featured Blog and News section for company updates.
* Detailed FAQ and interactive contact forms.

## 🏗 System Architecture
The system is built to handle complex logistics workflows, focusing on reducing manual entry errors through automation (auto-addressing, auto-pricing) and providing managers with tools for rapid border documentation.
